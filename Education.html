My Education Journey

Kindergarten - TADIKA SRI SELATAN

I began my educational journey in kindergarten, where I learned the basics of reading, writing, and socializing with my peers.

Duration: 2010 - 2011

Primary School - SK PENGKALAN TENTERA DARAT

I spent my early primary school years in SKPTD, where I developed a strong foundation in math, language, and science.

Duration: 2012 - 2017

Secondary School - SMK SUNGAI BESI

SMK Sungai Besi was my second home for five years. This is where I faced the challenges of SPM and learned the importance of discipline and perseverance in achieving my goals.

Duration: 2018 - 2022

Pra Diploma - UiTM LENDU

My time in Pre-Diploma at UiTM Lendu was short but incredibly meaningful. It was my first experience living away from home, making new friends from different states and give the confidence I needed to pursue my higher education.

Duration: 2023 - 2024

Diploma - UiTM SEGAMAT

My Diploma journey at UiTM Segamat was full of growth and learning. I didn't just gain academic knowledge, but I also learned the true meaning of teamwork, time management and resilience in completing my final year projects.

Duration: 2024 - 2026
